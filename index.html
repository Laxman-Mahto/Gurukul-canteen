About Us

Welcome to UEM/GURUKUL NIGHT CANTEEN! 
        Fuel up and unwind at Gurukul Night Canteen, the ultimate hangout spot for UEM campus students. Our cozy coffee shop serves a wide range of delicious treats to satisfy your cravings. Indulge in:

        - Specialty coffee drinks
        - A variety of biscuits to pair with your perfect cup
        - Crunchy snacks like Lays and Kurkure to munch on
        - Refreshing cold drinks to quench your thirst
        - Instant food items to curb your hunger
        
        At Gurukul Night Canteen, we're committed to serving fresh, quality food to fuel your mind and body. Whether you're studying for exams, socializing with friends, or just need a break from campus life, we've got you covered.
        
        Visit us tonight and experience the perfect blend of flavor, comfort, and camaraderie !
